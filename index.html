Privacy Policy (EU)

This policy is provided for transparency and is not legal advice.

1. Controller

Data controller: Kivana developer. Contact: privacy@kivana.app (replace before publishing).

2. Scope

This policy covers the Kivana marketing website and the Kivana desktop application. The desktop app is local‑first and stores data on your device.

3. Legal framework

We aim to follow the GDPR (Regulation (EU) 2016/679) transparency requirements and the EU ePrivacy rules for cookies/local storage.

4. Website data we process

  • Server logs (IP address, user agent, timestamps) processed by the hosting provider to deliver the website and protect it.
  • Local storage for language preference and pricing view (functional, no tracking).

We do not intentionally run analytics or advertising trackers on this website.

5. App data we process

  • Your financial records and attachments are stored locally on your device by default.
  • If you choose to use external AI providers, relevant text you submit may be sent to that provider as part of the request.

6. Purposes and legal bases (GDPR)

  • Website delivery and security: legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).
  • Functional preferences (language/billing view): legitimate interests or your request (Art. 6(1)(f) / Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Support communications (if you contact us): legitimate interests / contract (Art. 6(1)(f) / Art. 6(1)(b)).

7. Data retention

  • Website logs: retained by the hosting provider for a limited period for security/operations.
  • App data: stays on your device unless you export/share it.

8. Sharing and international transfers

  • Website hosting provider may process basic connection data to serve the site.
  • If you use external AI providers, processing may occur outside the EEA depending on the provider and your configuration.

9. Your rights (EEA/UK)

Depending on your location and circumstances, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection, and to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

10. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the website and recommend protecting your device and backups because the app stores data locally.

11. Changes

We may update this policy. Update the effective date when publishing changes.

FAQ
Is Kivana a web app or a desktop app?

Kivana is a desktop app. This website is only marketing and documentation.

Where is my data stored?

By default, your data is stored locally on your device.

Is the AI assistant required?

No. AI is optional. You can use local AI (Ollama) or disable AI entirely.

What’s the difference between Basic, Standard and Pro?

Basic is for organizing records and exporting to an accountant. Standard adds more tools plus basic AI. Pro is designed for accountant workflows, client packs, and advanced AI.

Can I attach invoices and bank statements?

Yes. Kivana can store attachments locally alongside your records.

Can I export for tax year periods?

Yes. The reporting workflow supports tax-year style reporting and export-ready summaries.

Do you track me on the website?

We do not intentionally run analytics or advertising trackers. Hosting providers may still log basic connection data.
